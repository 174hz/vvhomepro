VV HOME PRO: Vetted & Verified Operating Standards

To be authorized as a VV-Verified Partner, a contractor affirms that they meet, and will continually abide by, the following standards:

1. Build Elite Trust

Establish and maintain an exemplary track record of performance and professionalism.

  • A. Proven Stability: Actively providing services in their trade for at least two years prior to application, or providing verified documentation of similar experience under a different business entity.
  • B. Verified Credentials: Hold all required local, state, and provincial competency licenses, and maintain active, high-limit General Liability and Workers' Compensation insurance.
  • C. Ethical Standing: Be free from government action, litigation, or regulatory sanctions that demonstrate a failure to support VV HOME PRO’s principles of honesty and quality.

2. Advertise with Integrity

Follow established legal and ethical advertising practices, ensuring all representations are accurate and substantiated.

  • A. Accurate Scope: Clearly distinguish between services they directly perform and those they subcontract.
  • B. Trademark Usage: Use the VV HOME PRO "Vetted & Verified" badge only while in good standing, adhering strictly to the usage policies provided in the Partner Handbook.

3. Commit to Total Transparency

Openly disclose the business's nature, location, ownership, and all material facts affecting a customer's buying decision.

  • A. Immediate Disclosure: Promptly provide VV HOME PRO with documentation needed to verify compliance (licensing, insurance certificates, portfolio references).
  • B. Customer Clarity: Before work commences, provide customers with a clear, itemized scope of work, total cost (including tax/fees), direct contact information, and a clear warranty policy on craftsmanship.

4. Honor Commitments

Fulfill all contracts, promises, and representations in good faith.

  • A. Deadline Accountability: Make reasonable efforts to adhere to estimated start and completion dates, communicating delays proactively.
  • B. Quality Assurance: Ensure the completed project meets the quality standards agreed upon in the contract.

5. Proactive Responsiveness

Address all customer inquiries and disputes quickly, professionally, and in good faith.

  • A. Dispute Resolution: Participate in VV HOME PRO’s mediation process if a dispute with a homeowner cannot be resolved directly.
  • B. Professionalism: Address all significant issues raised by a client with appropriate documentation supporting the contractor's position.

6. Safeguard Information

Protect client data against unauthorized disclosure and respect customer privacy preferences.

  • A. Data Security: Ensure sensitive data (financial information, home access codes, personal addresses) is transmitted and stored securely according to industry standards.
  • B. Confidentiality: Respect customer preferences regarding contact frequency and do not share client information with third-party marketing services without explicit permission.

7. Embody Professionalism

Approach all business dealings with integrity, respecting the homeowner's property and time.

  • A. Job Site Conduct: Maintain a safe, clean, and professional job site.
  • B. Reputation Management: Avoid involvement in activities that reflect unfavorably on the contractor’s reputation or the reputation of the VV HOME PRO Registry.
