The Pinnacle Audit

Our proprietary technology and matching engine are governed by the Pinnacle Audit Standards. We don't just find contractors; we perform a deep-dive verification of the four pillars of trade reliability:

Verified Licensing

Every member must hold and maintain relevant Ontario licensing (ESA for Electrical, TSSA for Gas) to ensure legal compliance.

Insurance Audit

We verify comprehensive liability insurance and WSIB safety compliance annually to protect homeowners from project risk.

Proven Track Record

We analyze historical performance and trade references, ensuring a verifiable history of excellence in the community.

Accountability

Members commit to fair, transparent pricing and response times that set the gold standard for the industry.
