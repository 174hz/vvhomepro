Operating Standards

VV HOME utilizes a proprietary AI Matching Engine to bridge the gap between high-intent homeowners and verified service professionals.

1. Regional Optimization

Our engine is currently tuned exclusively for **Massachusetts, New Jersey, and Ontario**. Requests outside these zones are flagged for manual review to ensure service quality.

2. Lead Verification

Every project submitted is analyzed for "Germaneness." We only process leads related to structural home improvements, renovations, and essential repairs.

3. Data Integrity

We maintain a 1:1 matching ratio, ensuring your project details are only dispatched to the most relevant professionals in your specific trade zone.
