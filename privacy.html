Privacy Policy

Last Revised: February 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you affirmatively provide through our Matching Portal. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Project Details: Geographic location (Ontario), property type, and technical descriptions of the required trade work.
  • Interaction Data: Chat history with our AI Matching Engine.

2. How We Use Your Information

VVHP utilizes your information to facilitate the core mission of our service: matching you with a verified professional. Specifically:

  • To verify your location and match you with a nearby professional.
  • To provide the matched professional with the necessary context to provide an accurate quote.
  • To maintain records of verified matches for quality control and audit purposes.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third-party advertisers. Your information is shared only with the specific Vetted & Verified service providers matched to your project. We may also disclose information to governmental agencies where required by Ontario or Canadian Federal law.

4. Data Retention & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Project details are retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the matching request and satisfy our professional audit requirements.

5. Your Choices

You may request to view, correct, or delete your personal information from our matching database by contacting us at privacy@vvhomepro.com.
