The VV HOME PRO Mandate

VV HOME PRO operates as an independent trade verification body in Ontario. Our mission is to protect homeowners by maintaining a rigorous compliance database of licensed professionals. We bridge the critical gap between emergency repair requirements and verified technical expertise.

Compliance Protocol

01. Credential Audit Physical verification of TSSA (HVAC), Master Plumber, and Red Seal certifications.
02. Liability Assurance Ongoing monitoring of $2M+ commercial liability insurance and WSIB standing.
03. Background Check Evaluation of consumer protection standing and historical performance records.
04. Professional Standards Strict adherence to Ontario building codes and fixed-rate transparency.
